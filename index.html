Manual Traffic Monitoring in Kenya Today

«»

  • Can be inaccurate when there is heavy traffic

  • People counting traffic an be easily distracted

  • Pedestrian and bicycle traffic is not included

Semi-Manual Traffic Monitoring

«»

  • Create a mobile application

  • Advantages

    • Manual data entry once collection is complete not required

    • Press a button when vehicle goes by

  • Drawbacks

    • Still need two people

    • May miss vehicles when traffic is heavy
